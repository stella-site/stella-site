Sales Partnership

Details

With the growing number of companies using data-driven solutions for their marketing, sales and research, the need for quick and easy access to data is greater than ever. ParseHub provides an easy-to-use visual web scraper that allows users to scrape data from almost any website without the need of a developer or programmer.

Adding web scraping software to your product line-up is an excellent way of connecting your customers to the data they need. As a ParseHub Sales Partner, you can earn up to 30% commission on sales made.


How to Apply

Please contact us at hello@parsehub.com and let us know what type of partnership you are interested in, what services you currently offer and a little more about your business and we'll be in touch.
