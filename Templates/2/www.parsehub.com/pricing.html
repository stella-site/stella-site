What is a project?

One project typically scrapes data from one website. You can also combine data from multiple websites in a single project.

What is a run?

A project can be run multiple times, for example on different starting urls or at different times of day. All runs will crawl the target website at the same time as long as you are under the speed limit for your account.

What is the difference between private and public projects?

Private projects are not browseable by any other users. Public project designs and the data from their runs may be shared with other users.

What does the scraping speed per minute mean?

Each plan has a maximum speed limit on how fast ParseHub will collect the data. If you start enough runs to exceed the speed limit on your plan, they will be automatically queued until there is enough capacity.
